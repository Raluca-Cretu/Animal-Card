Lioness

Female lions are the pride's primary hunters. They often work together to prey upon antelopes, zebras, wildebeest, and other large animals of the open grasslands.

  • Scientific Name:: Panthera leo
  • Size: 4.5 to 6.5 ft
  • Weight: 265 to 420 lbs
  • Diet: Carnivores

Living in the grasslands, scrub, and open woodlands of sub-Saharan Africa, the lion is the second largest cat in the world. It is dwarfed slightly by the tiger, which is closely related and has a very similar body type. Unlike other cats, lions are very social animals.
